Who are We

About

Let us unlock child's potential together.

Smiles Foundation is part of a global charity which works for welfare of underprivileged children in Zambia. It is a registered Not-for-Profit Organization of the Registrar of Societies of Government of Zambia. Its mission is to unlock the potential of children, youths and women living in abject poverty through education, skill training and human rights protection enabling livelihood options for dignified living.

Smiles Foundation is a child-centered, community based development organization devoted to addressing the needs of children in Zambia. Smiles Foundation envisions a world where every child has a voice and the ability to achieve their full potential and it works to realize its mission to create a future of hope for children, families, and communities by helping them develop the skills and resources to overcome poverty and injustice.

As part of our Mission "Unlock Child's Potential", Smiles Foundation partners with local initiatives in establishing and managing programs on care, protection, development of children fallen in difficult circumstances (orphans and destitutes)

Our strategic priorities up to 2030 are:

  • children from broken homes
  • trafficked and abused children
  • street kids and child labor
  • children of HIV affected parents
  • children of single mothers
  • children heading homes
  • children forced into marriages.
