About Us

About

We are a non-profit charity ogranization.

Smiles Foundation is part of a global charity which works for welfare of underprivileged children in Zambia. It is a registered Non-Profit Organization of the Registrar of Societies of Government of Zambia. Its mission is to unlock the potential of children, youths and women living in abject poverty through education, skill training and human rights protection enabling livelihood options for dignified living.

Our Mission

Mission

Let us unlock child's potential together.

As part of our Mission "Unlock Child's Potential", Smiles Foundation partners with local initiatives in establishing and managing programs on care, protection, development of children fallen in difficult circumstances (orphans and destitutes):

Our strategic priorities up to 2030 are:

  • Child protection, empowerment and awareness
  • Youth participation, Sustainable livelihood and vocational skills development
  • Women empowerment
